- Thalassia an oceanic world with vibrant coral reefs beneath the galaxies clearest crystal water.
- This planet's civilization(s) are very of low galactic Intelligence.
- Inhabitants thrive on off planet funding into marine research with traditional fishing lifestyles.
- Inhabitants are not aware of interplanetary travel, all off world travelers say they are from "Manda"
- The Inhabitants evolved from fish to humans much faster than most other species.

- Note: The outpost "Manda" is a mapped phantom location, despite its nonexistence
